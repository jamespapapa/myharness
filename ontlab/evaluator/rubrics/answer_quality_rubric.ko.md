# Answer quality rubric

## Scored dimensions
- correctness
- evidence coverage
- traceability
- scope control
- abstention quality
- confidence calibration

## Strong answer
- states what is known
- points to concrete evidence
- makes uncertainty explicit
- avoids importing facts from seed repos
- avoids pretending a missing edge exists

## Bad answer
- hand-wavy architecture summary without evidence
- overconfident inference
- mixes target and seed facts
- ignores missing links or config/infrastructure uncertainty
